All About Buses

What is a Bus?

A bus is a large vehicle that carries passengers by road, typically one serving the public on a fixed route and for a fare.

Buses are a common form of public transportation.

Types of Buses

  • City Bus
  • School Bus
  • Tour Bus
  • Shuttle Bus

Fun Facts about Buses

  1. The first motorized bus was built in 1895.
  2. Buses can carry more passengers than cars.
  3. Some buses are powered by electricity or natural gas.
